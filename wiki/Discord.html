Rules

  • Keep the channels family friendly: No swear words or discussion of non G-rated topics.
  • No message spam: Wait a few seconds between each message, instead of sending multiple images directly to Discord, link an image gallery, etc.
  • Use the correct channels: Refer to the channel descriptions to find out if your message is going to be on-topic.
  • Respect others: Constructive critique is acceptable, trolling or personal attacks aren't.
  • No dangerous links: Any links sent with NSFW or malicious content will be removed.
  • Report users through DM: Refrain from talking to the user you wish to report, DM moderators instead.
  • English only: Sorry in advance, this rule is only here to aid our moderation.
  • Don't get banned: Alt accounts will also be banned.
