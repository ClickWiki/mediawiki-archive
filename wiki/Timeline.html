Timeline

List of events throughout Clickteam:

Past Events
  • 1993
 Yves and François establish Europress.
  • 1995
 Their first product, Klik & Play is released.
  • 1996
 Their second product, Click and Create is released.
  • 1996
 The Games Factory is released. "The world's first power tool to make your own computer games."
  • 1998
 Multimedia Fusion is released.
  • 2000
 Multimedia Fusion receives a minor update to 1.2.
  • 2001
 Multimedia Fusion receives a minor update to 1.5.
  • 2001
 Jamagic is released.
  • 2002
 Multimedia Fusion Pro is released.
  • June 2006
 The Games Factory 2 and Multimedia Fusion 2 is released.
  • 2008
 The Hardware Accelerated runtime is integrated to MMF2.
  • 2009
 The Java Exporter is integrated to MMF2.
  • 2009
 The Games Factory 2 and Multimedia Fusion 2 is translated to Japanese.
  • March 2010
 The Flash Exporter is released.
  • July 2011
 The iOS Exporter is released.
  • December 2011
 The Unicode Exporter is released.
  • March 2012
 A brand new Clickteam Chat Client called ClickConverse is now available.
  • June 2012
 The XNA Exporter is released.
  • April 2013
 The Android Exporter is released.
  • September 2013
 The Bug Tracker is now open!
  • November 2013
 Welcome Fernando to the Clickteam!
  • December 2013
 Clickteam Fusion 2.5 is released.
  • December 2013
 For the first time, Clickteam Fusion 2.5 and its exporters are available through Steam.
Welcome Danny to the Clickteam!
  • Early 2014
 The HTML5 Exporter is released.
  • October 2014
 Fused! - a new brand spanking new e-magazine is unveiled by Danny.
  • November 2014
 Welcome Ben to the Clickteam!
  • 2015
 The XNA Exporter becomes unsupported.
  • Christmas 2015
 Last issue of Fused! as an E-Magazine..
  • 2016
 Fused! is rebooted as a blog.
  • June 2016
 Fusion 2.5 Free Edition is released on Steam.
  • June 2016
 The UWP Exporter is released.
In the future...
  • 2017 (Source)
 Click Convention 2017.
  • 2017
The next generation of Fusion will be released.

Originally expected 2016.
Confirmed not 2016 via Facebook.

  • 2018
 Clickteam's 25th anniversary.
